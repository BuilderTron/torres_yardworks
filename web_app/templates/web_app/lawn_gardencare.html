Lawn & Garden Care

The team at Torres Yardworks understands the importance of a well kept lawn. Its an extension of the identity of your home or business. Thats why we provide a wide range of services. With years of experience and state-of-the-art equipment, Torres Yardworks can keep your landscape lush and beautiful all year long.

Torres Yardworks offer a wide variety of services to help your lawn and landscape reach their fullest potential and maintain it for years to come.

  • Sod Installation

  • Seasonal Fertilization
  • Seasonal Cleanup

Flower Bed Maintenance

If your yard has flower beds, gardens, or any other kind of accent landscaping, we can maintain them. From pulling weeds or cutting out unwanted plants, to maintaining soil and mulch, we can get your yard looking beautiful.
