Our History

Florentio Torres, owner of Torres Yardworks, began his work in landscaping in 2001. He says from that first project he knew it was a passion that would flourish into a life long career. He knew that as he learned more about the different types and styles of plants, what it took to make them thrive, and the value in caring for something living, that his journey would lead to a company of his own. In 2009, he founded Torres Yardworks and his love for the work has only grown! He says that many things give him satisfaction in his work, but most of all he loves the smile and amazed looks clients have at the end of each project. Seeing that big difference and knowing that he and his team were able to make something beautiful inspires him to keep going. His professional, fair, and honest work has been the key in growing his business by word of mouth alone. See what Torres Yardworks can do for you and your home!
